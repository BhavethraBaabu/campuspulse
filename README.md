- AI Chat — 285 Clark pages indexed, cited answers in 2 seconds
- Voice Input — speak your question, get your answer
- Live Deadline Countdowns — never miss add/drop, OPT, graduation
- AI Course Recommender — personalized course matches with AI reasoning
- GPA Calculator — Clark official 4.0 scale, instant results

- Study Planner — Finals, Registration, Graduation, OPT checklists
- Multi-language — English, Spanish, Hindi, Chinese, Arabic, French

- Campus Map — every building with directions
